Data Ingestion
09

ETL pipeline: extract, transform, load

ETL prepares a clean, reliable article record. AI enrichment happens after this ingestion stage.
E
ExtractRead RSS feeds and article pages from Tunisian news sources.
T
TransformNormalize the article fields and prevent duplicate URLs.
L
LoadSave the prepared article, ready for the AI enrichment stage.
The article URL is the unique key, so a later run updates the same story instead of creating a duplicate.

One article, made useful in four ways

The original story stays intact. Its added facts power what the reader sees in Tuniscope.
TN ORIGINAL ARTICLE
The original story is the source of truth
Title, source, date, image and full text stay together. Every result can be traced back to this article.
What those facts make possible
intfloat/multilingual-e5-small
Find related storiesEven when readers use different words.
cardiffnlp/twitter-xlm-roberta-base-sentiment
Read the tonePositive, neutral or negative coverage.
llama-3.1-8b-instant via Groq
Follow a themeEconomy, politics, sport and more.
Tunisia gazetteer + Groq fallback
See the placeGovernorate when a location is mentioned.
PostgreSQL + pgvector keeps the original article and the signals that make these experiences possible.

Enrichment Models
13

Two levels of enrichment

Some models explain one article. BERTopic reveals patterns across the whole collection.

For every article

Focused models add facts that readers can use right away.

Related storiesSemantic search connects similar coverage.
SentimentShows the tone of the coverage.
ThemeOrganises articles into clear subjects.
RegionPlaces coverage on the Tunisian map.

Across the whole collection

BERTopic groups related articles to surface recurring topics.

From individual stories to a view of what the news is talking about.

Orchestration & Tracking

The pipeline is scheduled, observable and safe to run again.
A completed Prefect flow makes every stage visible.
Runs every dayThe ETL flow starts automatically on schedule.
Starts on demandAn HTTP trigger supports a manual refresh when needed.
Recovers from errorsRetries protect the run when a source is temporarily unavailable.
16

Why MLflow matters

Topic discovery can change as articles and settings change. MLflow makes those changes explainable.
A real BERTopic run: settings, metrics and output are kept together.
Compare runsWhen the topics change, see whether the corpus or the clustering settings changed too.
Reproduce a resultKeep the embedding model, minimum topic size and output tied to the same run.
Choose with evidenceUse topic count and outlier share to identify a weak clustering result before it reaches the product.
